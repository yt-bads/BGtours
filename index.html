One of the exotic and beautiful places in Banten is Sawarna Beach. Because of its beauty, this beach is also called a hidden paradise in Banten. Sawarna Beach is located on Jl Sawarna Km. 12 No. 1, Sawarna, Bayah, Lebak Regency, Banten. The natural charm of this beach is very exotic.

Sawarna Beach is one of the most beautiful beaches in Banten Province, Indonesia. This beach has 6 beaches with different panoramas from each other. On this beach there is an old grave of a Dutchman named Jean Louis van Gogh. The distance from the coast from Jakarta is about 183 to 234 kilometers. The beaches that are most visited by tourists are Ciantir Beach and Tanjung Layar Beach, both of which are tourist icons of Sawarna Beach, the distance is crowded and the beaches are interconnected.
